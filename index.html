About

C&V Care Solutions Ltd. is a domiciliary care agency service that will provide individualised care packages to assist clients to remain in their own homes and encourage autonomy and where possible to be involved with self-care. C&V Care Solutions Ltd. is based in the northwest of England - West Midlands area.

C & V Care is an independent care company established in 2016 by Advanced Nurse Practitioner Janet Vernon who boasts many years of experience in the care and wellbeing of elderly patients. Her ultimate aim is to provide high standard domiciliary care for those in their later years.

Fees for the services offered are at competitive rates with several options for payments e.g. bank transfer, Paypal etc. For individual budgets or from direct payments. We aim to make the process of finding the correct care package and the appropriate carer as straight forward as possible.

We are a locally led service that offers excellent quality of care in your own home, supporting people to remain living at home, maintaining their independence and wellbeing.

Janet Vernon

Janet Vernon is the managing director of C&V Care Solutions, she is a registered nurse who possesses many years of experience working in the care industry e.g. NHS, Social Services. Her nursing qualifications were gained locally at the Wolverhampton Hospitals as far back as 1980 where she began as a trainee nurse. Upon working in the intensive care unit at Newcross Hospital for 7 years, her aspiration for developing her nursing skills further, required a new location. She took a post in the long term care of the elderly, an area of nursing that she thoroughly enjoyed and one that she believed set the scene for her to have a passion in delivering a high standard of care for older people who are known to sometimes have difficulties in accessing services.

Philosophy

The Agency aims to offer skilled care to enable people supported by us to achieve their optimum state of health and well-being.

Treat all people supported by us and all people who work here with respect at all times. Uphold the human and citizenship rights of all who work and visit here and of all Service Users.

Support individual choice and personal decision-making as the right of all Service Users.

Respect and encourage the right of independence of all Service Users.

Respect individual requirement for privacy at all times and treat all information relating to individuals in a confidential manner.
